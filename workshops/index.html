MATLAB Workshop

MATLAB is soon becoming an important asset to every Engineering student. This workshop will give a detailed explanation on the various facets of MATLAB right from the very basics. Handled by thorough professionals, this workshop will be extremely beneficial for students of all years.

MATLAB, along with its Simulink toolbox is fast becoming an essential software every engineering student irrespective of department must possess a knowledge of. Nowadays, it is hard to imagine any hardware implementation of control systems and power-based projects without initially simulating it on MATLAB and getting the desired output.

This year's MATLAB workshop will be conducted by thorough and experienced professionals from Educamp India. A student can enter the workshop with no prior knowledge on MATLAB and can leave with a strong understanding of the software. This workshop covers a wide range of topics starting right from the fundamentals, which we believe is the best way to go about it. The range of topics include M-File creation with programming, Power Electronics (Rectifiers and Converters) and Signals and Systems.

Don't miss out on this great opportunity to get yourself familiarised with this wonderful software and be a part of SSN's first ever integrated tech-fest, Invente.

Workshop - "Booming and Boom of IoT"

The chance to jump into the fast lane that's the Internet of Things (IoT) is an opportunity too good for many businesses to miss. This is particularly true in light of the UK government's budget pledge of nearly 140m to develop applications for the IoT and smart cities. India allocated Rs.500 cr to create 20 smart cities.

But as the IoT market size increases - research analyst Gartner predicted there will be 26 billion units by 2020 - hackers have an expanded surface area, and protecting company intellectual property (IP), customer data and operational infrastructures is more urgent than ever before.

As IoT transforms entire industries, creating tremendous benefits and new risks, Brisk Infosec already helps protect over a billion IoT devices. From smart televisions to critical infrastructure, we help securely protect and manage devices and communications, and deliver analytics to detect stealthy, sophisticated advanced threats to IoT systems.

Now IT industry is focusing on IoT, Brisk Infosec will be driving all attendees to IoT battle in the name of "Booming and Boom of IoT"

Workshop will flow through the topics

  1. About IoT and its existence
  2. How to create IoT Device and Application - Live
  3. Understanding functionalities and feature - Live
  4. Identifying critical vulnerabilities in IoT - Live
  5. Hacking IoT device - Live
  6. Security IoT device from attacks - Live
  7. Short Panel discussion
